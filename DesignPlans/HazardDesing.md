Hazard Spawner that spawns 3 different hazards that complicate the gameplay.

Hazards come down from the top of the screen. If they make contact with a fish, the fish is destroyed
and the player loses a life.

The player has to click the fish before they make contact with a hazard.

In addition to this feature we are planning on implementing another complication that when a fish
reaches the other side of the screen the player also loses a life. 

This way the game is not as easy and gives the player more of a challenge.
